Military Inventions

4th Century. Iron plate Armor. Long iron plates linked vertically to each other, was a feature of armor only found in Korea.

1380. Navel Artillery. Produced the most advanced naval cannons in East Asia.

1409. Hwacha. Mobile multiple rocket launcher. Used gunpowder to launch up to 200 singijeon rockets at once.

15th Century. Armored Warship. Geobukseon, or turtle ship was the first ever armored warship in the world. Most often associated with General Yi Sun-Shin

1592. Time Bomb. The pigyok chinchonllae, or the Flying Thunderbolt was a time bomb with an adjustable fuse mechanism.

1867. Bulletproof Vest. Myeonjae Baegab. A soft bulletproof vest invented n the Joseon Dynasty.

2010. Super aEgis II. An automated weapon capable of identification, tracking, and destruction of a moving target from 4km away.
