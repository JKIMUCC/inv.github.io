Agricultural Inventions

16,000 years ago. Rice Seed. World's oldest rice seed was discovered in South Korea.

1500 BC. Soybean Cultivation. First unambiguously domesticated, cultigen sized soybeans to be cultivated. Discovered in South Korea

1450s. Heated Greenhouse. First description of a heated greenhouse can be seen in the Sanga Yorok, in the section of cultivation during the winter.

15th Century. Rain Gauge. The cheugugi, the first standardized rain gauge. Korea was the only country to use a quantitative measuring device for meteorological purposes.
