Technological Inventions

1959. MOSFET. A semiconductor that is the basic element of the majority of electric products today. Invented by Dawon Kang and Mohamed Atalla at Bell Labs.

1988. Tablet Computer. The first tablet computer o be available commercially was the GRiDPad manufactured by Samsung.

1995. Contactless Smart Card. Seoul, South Korea was the first city in the world to utilize contactless smart cards for electronic ticketing.

1995. Kimchi Refridgerator. A refridgerator manufacture to match the requirements for storing kimchi. First commercially released by Winia Mando.

1997. MP3 Player. The first commercially available MP3 player was manufactured by SaeHan Information Systems in South Korea.

1999. MP3 Phone. First phone to allow MP3 playback was the SPH-M2100. Manufacture by Samsung.

1999. TV Phone. First TV phone was manufactured by Samsung. Called the SCH-M220

2000. Digital/Smart Refridgerator. The Internet Digital DIOS was first introduced by LG Electronics.

2001. Steam Mop. The first ever steam mop was invented by Romi Haan. Used to disinfect surfaces with hot steam.

2001/2002. Caller Ringback Tone. The CRBT was made to allow the user to choose their own personal ringtone. Developed by Witcom and launced by SK Telecom.

2004. HUBO. Second walking humanoid robot invented. First to move with natural gait.

2005. Albert Hubo. First walking humanoid robot with an android head.

2005. MAHRU. First networked-based walking humanoid robot in the world.

2009. 4DX. Invented by CJ Group. First 4D cinema technology. Augmented with environmental effects.

2010. LTE Phone. Samsung introduced the first LTE mobile and smartphone.

2010s. Retina Display. Developed by LG then later on bought by Apple.

2017. Method-2. First ever bipedal manned robot.

2019. 5G Smartphone. The first ever 5G smartphone was manufactured by Samsung. Galaxy S10 5G.
