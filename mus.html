Musical Inventions

6th Century. Gayageum. A traditional Korean stringed instrument inspired by the Chinese guzheng.

1047-1084. Janggu. A traditional korean drum. Most representative drum of Korean Music.

1397-1450. Musical Notation. Jeongganbo is a traditional musical notation system to represent rhythm, pitch, and time.

17th Century. Pansori. Korean Folk Opera. traditional genre of narrative song. Inscribed in the Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity by UNESCO.

1992. K-POP. Started with debut of Seo Taeji and Boys. Challenged musical and societal norms of South Korea by performing music influenced by American Genre.
